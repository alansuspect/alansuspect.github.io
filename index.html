About

Hello my name is Alan Quirk, a Freelance Web & Graphic Designer based on the Gold Coast, Australia with over 10 years experience.

Originally from the Isle of Man, Great Britain, I now call Australia home and I bring my years of freelance and professional experience in UI design, mobile/responsive design and front-end Wordpress development.

My services include a full freelance website build from consultation to design, development and creative direction of the project as well as completion and support beyond.

Rhodes Capalaba Website

A real estate brochureware website for a brand new apartment block in the heart of Capalaba.

A new website was required to replace the old and outdated current one. The idea was to really sell the apartments so we worked with large imagery and bold text to draw the viewer in. The site was built in Wordpress with a completely custom theme and is responsive making it mobile friendly.

The new website has increased the visitor rate by over 50% and analytics show each visitor is remaining on each page for longer. On top of this an easier to use contact form has improved sales leads dramatically.

Adam Berry Illustration

New portfolio and shop website featuring the work of Manx illustrator Adam Berry.

I have been helping Adam with his website redesigns since I built his first site back in 2003. This time he wanted to update the 5 year old design and give it a more modern feel.

Taking inspiration from various portfolios that use the ‘masonry’ effect of displaying images we set about constructing a website that could showcase his work in an interesting but accessible way. Being able to filter the type of artwork was also important in both his portfolio gallery and on the shop, where he wanted to continue selling prints, canvases and other merchandise.

Going off what Adam has requested I kept the site clean and simple to let the artwork shine, this is the focus of the website so nothing should get in the way of viewing his work. The site is also responsive and the masonry effect cascades down nicely to smaller devices.

I am really happy with the outcome of this website, especially as we were working towards a tight deadline. The website will be available soon at adamberry.net

  • Client: Adam Berry
  • Category: Web Design
  • URL: adamberry.net

Manx Cider Co

Website for the Manx Cider Company featuring their products and an online shop.

Isle of Man Maritime Group

A website and marketing brochure promoting the new Maritime Group.

The Isle of Man Maritime Group was formed in 2014 and required a website that would both promote the new group and also help promote and register interest in the Festival of Business 2014 event in which they were hosting various events.

The project required branding, print and online materials, including a brochure, website and event marketing materials.

Compass Ferry Software

A parallax-style brochure website for PDMS' new ticketing and tracking Compass Ferry Sofware.

The Ferry Software website was in need of a redesign following PDMS’ acquisition of certain parts of the business and assets of Maxima Information Group Limited.

Initially the design was undertaken by an external design team, however the process was moved in-house and built on the initial brief of a fully responsive website that stood out from the competitors.

I took the branding and reworked some initial designs, keeping the established colours and the idea of parallax scrolling, and designed and built the HTML and CSS for a responsive, modern site that showcases the product.
